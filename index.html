Frances (or Francie) is a student at Connecticut College, located in New London, Connecticut. She lives in the Bay Area and has a twin sister. She is majoring in both computer science and gender, sexuality, and intersectionality studies. She has a passion for working with children, which she's explored through programs like Peninsula Bridge as well as the Connecticut College Lab School.
Along with being a full time student, Francie is also employed by a private chef who created Farm Fresh Kitchen. She works with him on the weekends to create 15-20 meals for families around the Bay Area. Another hobbie of hers is playing with makeup. It sparks her creativity and gives her time to refocus and ground herself.
